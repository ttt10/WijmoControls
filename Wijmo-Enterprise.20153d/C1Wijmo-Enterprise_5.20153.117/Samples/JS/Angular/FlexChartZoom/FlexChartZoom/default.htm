FlexChart Zoom

This page shows how to zoom Wijmo's FlexChart.

  • Mouse action:
    1. Zoom mode: Select the chart area to zoom in, or scroll mouse wheel to zoom in/out.
    2. Pan mode: Click and drag within the chart area to change the display range.
  • Touch action:
    1. Pan the chart area to change the display range; pinch it to zoom in/out.
