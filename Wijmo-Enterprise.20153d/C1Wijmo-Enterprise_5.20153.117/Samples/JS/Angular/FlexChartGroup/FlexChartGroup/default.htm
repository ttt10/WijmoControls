FlexChart Group & Drill Down

This page shows how to show Wijmo's FlexChart control with group data.

Step 1) Select how would you like to group the data: Country and City Country and Year Year and Country Country , City and Year Year, Country and City
Step 2) Select how would you like to aggregate the data: Sum Count of Non-Null Average Maximum Minimum Deviation Standard Deviation Variance Population Standard Deviation Population variance
Step 3) Select how would you like to show the data: Column Pie
Step 4) Click a column, sector or legend to drill down into the details, or click on the header hyperlinks to return to a higher level.
